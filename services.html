Our Services

Display support built around your venue, content, and audience.

Linx Media helps you turn presentations, live feeds, worship moments, brand visuals, and stage content into a clear viewing experience for everyone in the room.

01

LED Screen Rentals

Rent bright LED screens for indoor halls, outdoor programs, stage backdrops, overflow viewing, product reveals, and sponsor visibility.

We help choose display size and placement so your visuals are readable from the right distance and angle.

02

LED Wall Setup & Installation

Our crew assembles, aligns, configures, and tests LED wall panels so the screen is stable before the program starts.

For longer-term display needs, we can also support planning and setup for halls, churches, offices, and event spaces.

03

Sound Systems

We provide sound support for speeches, panels, worship, music, ceremonies, and indoor or outdoor gatherings.

Our team matches microphones, speakers, monitors, and operators to the size and shape of the audience area.

04

Stage Production

We help coordinate the relationship between the stage, LED screen, presenters, performers, cameras, and media sources.

This keeps the program visually organized, whether the event is formal, celebratory, corporate, or worship-focused.

05

Conference & Exhibition Support

We support conferences, summits, exhibitions, and trade events with screens for slides, speaker visuals, sponsor loops, and live camera feeds.

Our technicians help keep sessions visible, branded, and easy for attendees to follow.

06

Event Media Playback

We handle the details that sit behind the screen: videos, slides, countdowns, holding screens, sponsor graphics, program cues, and display switching.

This helps organizers avoid awkward pauses and keeps the audience focused on the moment.

07

Fixed Display Planning

For venues that need a more regular display system, we assist with screen planning, placement advice, configuration, and operational guidance.

We focus on practical viewing needs, maintenance access, and day-to-day usability.

08

Technical Crew & On-Site Support

Equipment is only useful when someone capable is watching it. Our crew manages setup checks, live operation, troubleshooting, and breakdown.

You get a technical team that stays close to the program and responds quickly when something changes.
